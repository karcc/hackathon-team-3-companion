How to Play

Welcome: {{user.nickname}}

  • Explore the museum while answering questions along the way!
  • Use the image above each question as a clue for where the answr can be found.
  • Select the answer that you feel is correct before time runs out!
  • Click "Send Answer" to submit your answer for that particular question:
    • If the answer is incorrect and if time allows, you can attempt to answer again. Note: You will be penalized 30 points for every incorrect answer submitted.

    • If desired, you may skip the question by clicking the "Pass" button. You will forfeit all of your points for this question.

    • If time runs out, you will receive 0 points and will be moved to the next question.
  • Scores are tallied throughout the game and are determined by how quickly you were able to correctly answer each question.
  • Your final score will be displayed on the leadership board at the end of the game!
